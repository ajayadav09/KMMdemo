KMM for Students

We present Keep Moving Movemet 2013 for the Class X
students in your school to build their future and to build our nation
that must lead the world. We do so with complete gratitude.


Inspirational living is all about developing 'High Self Esteem' and
practising daily 'Courage'. Courage, not to be rebel but the
courage, to do, what is 'right'!


Inspiration, imagination, insights, experiences
tools, techniques, games, achievements-
contribute in making 'High Self Esteem' not
just a concept but a belief.


By engaging the students by all of the above, we in KMM need
your permission to lead our class X students on the road to 'High
Self Esteem'


While the teachers at school are doing the incredible job of
teaching the minds, the teaching of the heart is becoming the
work of the specialists.

KMM for Teachers

A sculptor uses both strength and deft touched to chip a bock of
stone to create a Masterpeice

An Artist uses soft stroke of his brush to create a wonder.

A scientist works on both, manmade and natural surroundings to Discover and Invent.

We believe, teachers combine all of these and much more in their work, year
after year and decades after decades!


At Lifeschool Foundation, we believe that until the "Feeling of Inspiration" does not
touch us, life is incomplete.


Because Inspiration is what fills our minds with imagination.
Beacuse Inspirationis what fills our hearts with passion
Because Inspiration is what fills our bodywitha drive to do, to perform.


It is the teachers who make us "Feel Inspired" History is replete with examples of
great souls who have been shaped and modeled by their Teachers, whether it be
great scientific minds, intellectuals or even sports personalities. We believe Teachers
create History and also the Future! The Teachers hat are creed that has selflessly
devoted itself to such a monumental and important work.


Keeping this in mind, we launched Keep Moving Movement(KMM) in 2010-11. At
one end. we wanted to express gratitude to the relentless work the teachers are
doing to create the children for the future. On the other end, we wished to interact
and together have an impactful learning.


We believe in the concept of "Learning to Teach and Teaching to learn!"


In 2010-2011, we directly touched lives of about 7500 children and 3000 Teachers. In
2011-12 we would have directly touched lives of about 5000 teachers and through
them atleast 100000 children. We strongly feel that goodness must spread. For
the coming year, we have up the ante. We intend to touch about 100000 teachers
directly and hemce by millions of children.
